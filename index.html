02 / Artistic Background

My Artistic Journey

🎸

Guitar

The guitar was my first love — an instrument that transformed a simple melody into a story. I play both acoustic and electric, exploring genres from fingerstyle classical to rock and pop. The versatility of the guitar lets me express every emotion I feel, from quiet introspection to powerful energy.

Piano

The piano is my second artistic language. Its ability to produce both melody and harmony simultaneously fascinates me. Through the piano, I explore classical pieces, contemporary ballads, and improvisational jazz. Each key press is an intentional decision, a brushstroke of sound.

06 / Artistic References

Those Who Inspire Me

Every artist stands on the shoulders of those who came before. These are the musicians and creators who shaped my artistic voice.

🎸

John Mayer

Blues / Pop / Rock

A guitar virtuoso whose soulful playing blurs the lines between genres. His blues-influenced fingerstyle work deeply inspired my approach to the guitar.

🎹

Ludwig van Beethoven

Classical

The titan of classical piano composition. Beethoven's Moonlight Sonata was the first piece that made me understand music as pure emotional architecture.

🎵

Hans Zimmer

Film Score / Orchestral

The master of cinematic soundscapes. Zimmer proves that music can paint vivid images without a single word — an inspiration for how I approach musical storytelling.

🎸

Tommy Emmanuel

Fingerstyle / Acoustic

Watching Tommy play guitar feels like witnessing a full orchestra in a single instrument. His fingerstyle techniques transformed how I see the acoustic guitar's potential.
